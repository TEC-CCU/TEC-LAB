Game 1: Hit the target

A particle is launched toward a target. Students only adjust speed and angle, then learn how projectile range depends on initial conditions.

Game 2: Torque tuner

Students tune torque and time to make a rotating disk reach a target angular speed. This makes angular acceleration and work-energy ideas easier to see.

Learning focus: for a fixed disk, a larger torque gives a larger angular acceleration. A longer push time also increases final angular speed.

Game result

Match the target angular speed on the gauge as closely as possible.

Game 3: Energy ramp race

A rolling rigid body moves down a ramp. Students change the height and body radius, then predict whether the final speed becomes larger or smaller.
