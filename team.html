Principal Investigator

Dr. Anurag Dahiya

Assistant Professor
Department of Mechanical Engineering
National Chung Cheng University

Dr. Dahiya's research focuses on chemical kinetics of fuel combustion and thermal-fluid design of heat exchangers using advanced CFD techniques. His work combines detailed reaction mechanisms with high-fidelity simulations to improve energy system efficiency and safety.

Building Our Research Team

The TEC Lab is currently in its early stages of formation. We are actively recruiting talented and motivated MS and PhD students to join our research group and contribute to cutting-edge thermal-fluid engineering research.

Join Our Lab!

We are seeking passionate graduate students interested in:

  • Chemical kinetics and combustion modeling
  • Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD)
  • Thermal management systems
  • Heat exchanger design and optimization
  • AI/ML applications in thermal engineering

What We Offer:

  • Hands-on experience with industry-standard software (ANSYS, COMSOL, OpenFOAM)
  • Opportunity to publish in high-impact journals
  • Structured 2-year MS or 3-4 year PhD program
  • Strong foundation in both theory and simulation
  • Career preparation for industry and academia
